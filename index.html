Digital financial services have become the new normal. Going beyond the digital strategy is key to success (or survival) in the next two years. KEYNOTE AND FEATURED SPEAKERS
 
 
Scott Bales is a global leader in the cutting edge arena known as The Digital Shift encompassing innovation, design and mobility in a world gone digital. As a futurist and digital strategist, he thrives on the intersection between cultural and behavioral changes in the face of technology advancement. Originally from Australia, Bales runs the innovation firm Innovation Labs Asia, has led corporate startup collaborations, is a keynote speaker at TEDx and authored the books Mobile-Ready and Innovation Wars.
 
 
Michael Araneta is Research Director for IDC Financial Insights in APAC and country manager for IDC Thailand. He leads the APAC financial services practice and is a thought leader across a broad range of strategic and tactical issues in Asian banking. Araneta has consulted clients on mergers and acquisitions of banks in the region, digital transformation, Big Data, cloud in banking, product innovation strategies, customer centricity and CRM initiatives, vendor product portfolio positioning, and core banking systems strategies.
 
 
Cyrus Daruwala is Managing Director for IDC Financial Insights in APAC. For the past 20 years, he has been working with financial institutions to help them assess their business and operational and technical challenges, select the right vendors/partners, better understand their IT TCO, and grow their customer base.
 
 
Nic Parmaksizian is a director of Capco’s global banking practice, based in London, specializing in customer experience design and implementation. He co-leads the new banking consumer area and formerly held senior roles in Barclays retail division.
